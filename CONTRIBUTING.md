# Contributing

This repository is a sanitized public documentation layer.

Contributions are welcome when they improve clarity, safety, structure, or conceptual usefulness without exposing private material.

## Contribution principles

- Keep examples fictional.
- Avoid personal data.
- Do not include private prompts or real checkpoints.
- Do not reference private paths, drives, machines, or credentials.
- Prefer conceptual clarity over operational exposure.

## Good contributions

- clearer documentation;
- safer terminology;
- generic templates;
- improved glossary entries;
- public architecture explanations;
- privacy and security improvements.

## Not accepted

- real private files;
- raw chat exports;
- secrets;
- internal operational procedures;
- sensitive personal history;
- material copied from the private core.
